ONYX KOL Affiliate Partnership Agreement

1. Partnership Overview: ONYX will provide the Affiliate (KOL) with a promotional tracking link and/or promo code to promote ONYX's premium private charter and transfer services in Taiwan.

2. Customer Offer: Customers who book an 8-hour private charter through the ONYX official website using the Affiliate's link/code will be entitled to 1 complimentary additional hour of service.

3. Commission Structure: For every eligible booking, the Affiliate will earn a ten percent (10%) commission based on the order's amount excluding tax. An eligible booking is defined as: a booking made explicitly via the Affiliate's specific tracking link/code where the customer has successfully completed their travel itinerary, and no refunds or transaction disputes have occurred.

4. Tracking and Attribution: Commissions will only be credited for orders explicitly tracked through the Affiliate's assigned promotional link or code.

5. Payout Terms:

  • Commissions are calculated based on itineraries completed in the previous calendar month.
  • Payments will be disbursed on the 20th day of the month following the completion of the customer's itinerary.
  • Tax Withholding: Applicable income tax owed by the Affiliate will be legally withheld in accordance with Taiwan’s tax regulations prior to the commission payout (if applicable). The actual amount received by the Affiliate will be the net amount after statutory tax deductions.
  • All payments will primarily be processed via PayPal. However, ONYX reserves the right to use alternative payment methods (such as Payoneer or direct bank transfer) upon mutual agreement to ensure timely payouts. The Affiliate is responsible for providing a valid PayPal account initially.
