We strive to provide the best premium chauffeur experience. To ensure vehicle and driver availability for all our clients, we strictly adhere to the following cancellation policy:

  • Cancellations made 7 days (168 hours) or more prior to the scheduled pick-up time: 100% Full Refund.
  • Cancellations made between 3 to 7 days (72 to 168 hours) prior to the scheduled pick-up time: 50% Refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 3 days (72 hours) prior to the scheduled pick-up time, or No-Shows: No Refund (0%).

Force Majeure (Weather & Flight Disruptions)

In the event of flight cancellations due to severe weather (e.g., typhoons) or other force majeure events, please contact us immediately. We will offer a free rescheduling or a full refund upon verification of the official flight cancellation status.

How to Request a Cancellation

To cancel your booking or request a refund, please contact us directly via Email or WhatsApp. Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within 14-28 business days.
